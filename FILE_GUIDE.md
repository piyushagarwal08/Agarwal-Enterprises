## 🔧 Customization Quick Reference

### Change WhatsApp Number
**File:** index.html or index-full.html  
**Search:** `wa.me/919057529646`  
**Replace:** With your number (with country code)

### Change Business Name
**File:** Both HTML files  
**Search:** `Agarwal Enterprises`  
**Replace:** With your business name

### Change Colors (Green Theme)
**File:** Both HTML files  
**Search:** `:root { --primary-green:`  
**Replace:** With your hex codes

### Add/Edit Products
**File:** products.json or CSV upload  
**Add:** New product entries  
**Update:** Prices via CSV

- ❌ Don't change product names
